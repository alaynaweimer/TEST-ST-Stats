📌 How does this tracking system work?

⚠️ Stats are calculated weekly from IntakeQ data by your Lead. Do not edit these fields directly.
  • QCompleted: Billable hours including all credits as of the most recent weekly update.
  • Client Hours: Treatment + evaluation only — used for bonus eligibility. PTO and holidays do NOT count.
  • QCHE Formula: (52 − weeks of leave) × weekly hrs ÷ 4 = quarterly 100% target
  • Quarterly Expectations: Weekly hrs × 13 weeks. Met via billable hours + PTO/holiday credits.
  • Bare Minimum: 96% of QCHE must be met to remain at your current level.
  • Holiday credits = Weekly expectation ÷ 5

📈 Bonus Structure

  • Tier 1 (1–10 hrs over QCHE): $30 per client hour for the first 10 hours above 100% QCHE. Max $300.
  • Tier 2 (11+ hrs over QCHE): $50 per client hour for every hour beyond the first 10 — no upper limit!
  • Bonuses stack — first 10 hrs over QCHE earn $30/hr ($300), then $50/hr for every additional hour.
  • Use the Projection Scenario calculator on your Stats page to model outcomes.

🔄 Changing Your Pay Track

  • You may change your pay track once per quarter, before the quarter starts.
  • Contact your Lead to request a track change for the upcoming quarter.
